### Civil Procedure: Law Applied by Federal Courts

#### 1

A man brought a diversity action against a pharmaceutical company in a federal district court in State A. In his complaint, the man alleged that the company violated State A's false advertising law. The company moved to dismiss the man's action for failure to state a claim on the ground that the false advertising law does not apply to advertising by pharmaceutical companies.

Several lower state courts in State A have recognized the company's defense. The highest state court in State A has never ruled or noted in dicta whether this defense is valid. Based on the lower state courts' decisions, the federal district court ruled that the company's defense applies and granted its motion to dismiss. The man appealed.

State A law permits a federal district court to certify an unsettled question of state substantive law to the state's highest court for clarification.

Is the federal appellate court likely to overturn the dismissal?

一名男子在A州的联邦地区法院对一家制药公司提起了多元管辖权诉讼。在他的诉状中，该男子声称该公司违反了A州的虚假广告法。该公司以虚假广告法不适用于制药公司的广告为由，以未能陈述有效诉因为由，请求驳回该男子的诉讼。

A州的数个下级州法院已认可该公司的抗辩。A州的最高州法院从未就该抗辩是否有效作出裁决或在判决附论中提及。基于下级州法院的裁决，联邦地区法院裁定该公司的抗辩适用，并批准了其驳回动议。该男子提起了上诉。

A州法律允许联邦地区法院将一个未决的州实体法问题移交州最高法院进行澄清。

联邦上诉法院是否可能推翻该驳回裁定？

A. No, because the federal district court properly relied on the rulings issued by the state's lower courts.
不会，因为联邦地区法院恰当地依据了州下级法院作出的裁决。

B. No, because the federal district court's decision was not clearly erroneous.
不会，因为联邦地区法院的判决并无明显错误。

C. Yes, because the federal district court abused its discretion when it dismissed the action.
会，因为联邦地区法院在驳回诉讼时滥用了其自由裁量权。

D. Yes, because the federal district court failed to certify the issue to the highest state court in State A.
会，因为联邦地区法院未能将该问题移交A州的最高州法院。

Under State B's choice-of-law rules, State B law applies to all state substantive law claims.

一名居住在A州的原告起诉其前雇主，索赔300万美元，指控其违反联邦法律进行就业歧视、违反其雇佣合同以及侵犯其专利权。该诉讼在位于B州的联邦法院提起，B州是该雇主的公司注册州和主要营业地。

#### 6

The contract between the bakery and the mill contained a clause designating State B courts as the sole venue for litigating disputes arising under the contract. Under precedent of the highest court in State A, forum-selection clauses are unenforceable as against public policy; under U.S. Supreme Court precedent, such clauses are enforceable.

The mill has moved to transfer the case to a federal court in State B, citing the forum- selection clause in the parties' contract and asserting the facts that the flour was produced in State B and that the majority of likely witnesses are in State B.

Is the court likely to grant the mill's motion?

一家在A州注册成立且总部位于A州的面包店，与一家在B州注册成立且总部位于B州的面粉厂，就面粉厂向面包店交付的面粉质量发生了争议。面包店以违约为由，在A州的联邦法院起诉了该面粉厂，寻求10万美元的损害赔偿。

面包店和面粉厂之间的合同中包含一项条款，指定B州法院为合同项下争议的唯一诉讼地点。根据A州最高法院的先例，法院选择条款因违反公共政策而不可执行；而根据美国最高法院的先例，此类条款是可执行的。

面粉厂已动议将案件转移至B州的联邦法院，其理由是双方合同中的法院选择条款，并主张面粉在B州生产且大多数可能的证人都在B州。

会，因为联邦法律管辖审判地的转移，并且在B州进行诉讼对证人和当事人来说更为便利。

#### 7

Ten months after surgery in a hospital, a patient who had suffered complications from the surgery sued the surgeon and the hospital in federal court for medical malpractice, seeking $750,000 in damages. Timely personal service was made on the surgeon and the hospital. Two months later, during discovery, the patient learned that the hospital was owned by a national health-care company and moved to amend the complaint to substitute the company for the hospital.

The company moved to dismiss, arguing that the forum state had enacted a one-year statute of limitations for medical malpractice actions and that the company had been served after the limitations period had expired. The company also noted that the state's highest court has interpreted the limitations statute as forbidding any relation back of amendments adding parties in medical malpractice actions. The patient argued that the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure control, and that they allow relation back under the circumstances of this case.

Which law governs whether relation back will be permitted under these circumstances?

在一家医院接受手术十个月后，一名因手术出现并发症的患者，以医疗事故为由在联邦法院起诉了外科医生和该医院，索赔75万美元。诉状已及时亲自送达外科医生和医院。两个月后，在证据开示（discovery）期间，患者得知该医院为一家全国性医疗保健公司所有，遂请求修改起诉状，以该公司替代该医院为被告。

该公司请求驳回起诉，理由是法院地州已针对医疗事故诉讼制定了一年的诉讼时效法规，而该公司在诉讼时效届满后才收到诉状送达。该公司还指出，该州最高法院已将该诉讼时效法规解释为禁止在医疗事故诉讼中，为追加当事人而作的诉状修改可以追溯适用（relation back）。患者则辩称，应以 Federal Rules of Civil Procedure 为准，且根据该规则，在本案情形下允许追溯适用。

在这种情况下，应适用何种法律来决定是否允许追溯适用？

A. Federal law, because the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure govern over conflicting state rules that deny relation back.
联邦法律，因为 Federal Rules of Civil Procedure 优先于那些否认追溯适用的、与之冲突的州法律规则。

B. Federal law, because the state law on relation back is common law and federal courts are bound only by state statutory law.
联邦法律，因为关于追溯适用的州法律是判例法，而联邦法院仅受州成文法的约束。

C. State law, because statutes of limitation are substantive and state law controls substantive matters.
州法律，因为诉讼时效是实体性问题，而州法律管辖实体性事项。

D. State law, because the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure authorize the use of state law for relation back.
州法律，因为 Federal Rules of Civil Procedure 授权使用州法律处理追溯适用问题。
